# 📊 Sales Performance & Profitability Analysis (2022-2025)

This project explores a multi-region sales dataset to identify key drivers of profit, regional performance trends, and customer purchasing behavior using **Python** and **Seaborn**.

## 🛠️ Technologies Used
- **Python**: Core analysis.
- **Pandas**: Data manipulation and cleaning.
- **Seaborn & Matplotlib**: Advanced data visualization.
- **Jupyter Notebook**: Interactive development environment.

## 🔍 Business Insights Explored
1. What is the correlation between Sales and Profit?
2. Which region is the most profitable in terms of efficiency?
3. How does profit fluctuate over time across different regions?
4. What is the distribution of order quantities?

## 💡 Key Findings
- **Profit Efficiency**: The **South** region shows the highest profit margins for large-scale sales compared to other regions.
- **Seasonal Trends**: A recurring significant dip in profit was observed in **May** across all regions, suggesting a seasonal market slowdown.
- **Sales vs. Profit**: A strong positive correlation exists, but profit variance increases significantly as sales volume grows.
